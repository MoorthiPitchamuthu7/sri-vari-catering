About Sri Vari Catering

Founded in 2010, Sri Vari Catering has been serving authentic South Indian vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisine with traditional taste and high standards of hygiene. Over the years, we have successfully catered hundreds of weddings, family functions, and corporate events across the region.

Our commitment to fresh ingredients, timely service, and rich flavours has earned us the trust of our customers. Whether it is a grand wedding or a small family gathering, we ensure every event is memorable with quality food and professional service.
